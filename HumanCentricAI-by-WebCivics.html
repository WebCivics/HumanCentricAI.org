Human Centric AI

Human Centric AI is technology in the form of inter-personal computing software ecosystems, that supports personal and private AI Agents and related Services.

 The Human Centric AI initiative by Web Civics, provides community resources for cooperative & collaborative activities to progress our Humanitarian capacity to define, produce and make available Human Centric Artificial Intelligence Ecosystems.

Personal AI

Human Centric AI is built upon a series of foundational layers that are required to provide Personal & Private AI Agents that are owned by the Natural Person whom it serves. 
This is considered to be a foundational requirement, that continues to be absent.
One of the ways this is sought to be achieved is via a Related Project called "Webizen" that will in future be a form of personally owned, HumanCentric AI Agent supported by the systems needed for Human Centric AI Apps.

This initiative is part of the broader community of practice methodologies to prioritise the empowerment of people and communities, whilst addressing serious problems that give rise to many very serious forms of harms being experienced without safety protocols or tools to seek lawful remedy. Human Centric AI works to provide an instrumental toolset to address those problems in a manner that empowers people via Law.

Permissive Commons

'Commons' information must essentially be decentralised and able to be used privately. Commons Information is not simply about public 'open data'; but moreover, about all sorts of informatics that are shared in some way, shape or form between agents, that are subject to various different kinds of rules.

Human Centric AI requires Human Centric Digital Identity & the Ability to ensure Human Beings can Manage Agreements between One-another.

The ability to produce Human Centric AI systems, is predicated upon the ability for persons who are lawfully entitled to form agreements with one-another are made able to do so in a lawfully enforcable manner; both, on behalf of themselves & in relation to dependants for whom they may have guardianship responsibilities to support & protect. 

To get the basic infrastructure implemented and made operational in a manner that supports and preserves the meaningful purpose and utility of various internationally recgonised statements about our shared values, throughout our diverse diaspora of constituencies of our interconnected Human Family, we must find the means to promote and support the development of Peace Infrastructure that empowers us all, to support ourselves & one another. If our voices are to be heard, we must have the tools that we need to provide verifiable claims in courts of law as necessary.

Organisational Involvement Opportunities

Due to the nature of what it is that we seek to achieve, we would prefer participants to be engaged on their own private basis.   
This does not mean t​hat we are not interested in representatives of organisations who may want to get involved.  
If there are members of your organisation who would benefit from being involved and thereby be better equipped to share those benefits with your broader organisational or institutional community; then they're most welcome on the basis that they are able and/or entitled to be involved in their personal capacity or any other capacity that is able to agree to the terms outlined by the instruments referred to on the Values Credentials Resource.  Whilst institutional and/or organisational support of some kind is absolutely necessary, the solutions engineered by these works will not seek to bind any person to any platform or organisation other than as is required by law; in a manner that is compatible with the rights and responsibilities otherwise outlined by law. 

If your organisation wants get involved please feel most welcome to get in touch and let us know.
